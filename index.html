Welcome, to a page dedicated to investigating the opinions of others! Below you will find (hopefully)interesting analysis and statistics about the FCC's released comments archive.

Sentiment Analysis with Python

Using nltk a python natural language toolkit, I used the movie_review text classifier (73-80% accuracy, same as a human) to guage the positive and negative wordings of the comments made by people to the FCC. Unsurprisingly, most of them are negative as a great many people who have written in want realNet Neutrality.
